# Folder Structure

## Core Platform
- `backend/`
  - `auth/` (user registration and login)
  - `profiles/` (user profile management)
  - `videos/` (video uploading and processing)
  - `looping/` (seamless looping logic)

## AI-Powered Features
- `ai/`
  - `background_removal/` (AI models for background removal)
  - `virtual_environments/` (virtual background options)

## Social Engagement
- `social/`
  - `collabs/` (collaborative video features)
  - `challenges/` (challenge and trend features)

## UI/UX Design
- `frontend/`
  - `components/` (React/Vue.js components)
  - `styles/` (CSS/SCSS for styling)
